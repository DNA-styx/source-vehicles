Mounting & Dismounting

Approach the vehicle and press E to get in or out. Do not try to exit while the vehicle is moving.

Driver

The first soldier to board takes the wheel. You can carry one passenger in the second seat.

Passenger

If a driver is already aboard, press E near the vehicle to ride as a passenger. Most small arms can be used from the passenger seat.

Machine guns, grenades and rockets are disabled while riding.
Vehicle Damage

Vehicles absorb damage and will eventually be destroyed. If yours is taking heavy fire, get clear. A wrecked vehicle is a hazard to anyone nearby.
